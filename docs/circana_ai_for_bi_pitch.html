01Executive Thesis

Circana sits on the largest commercial dataset in CPG and General Merchandise. The unsolved problem in market insights today is the last mile: turning that data into a brand manager's, category leader's, or customer-team's next action in seconds — with auditable, defensible insight. Liquid AI is Circana's horizontal GenAI platform; the white-space opportunity is the verticalized, persona-shaped analyst surfaces that sit on top of it. This brief proposes a proven three-layer AI-for-BI reference — already running end-to-end, retargetable to CPG, FMCG, and GM in a single quarter — that lights up five immediately monetizable use cases.

The market gap

CPG and GM brand teams generate enormous POS, panel, media, and price data but lack the tools to extract insight at the speed decisions require. The average time from a share-shift signal to a management decision is 2–3 weeks.

What we offer

A working three-layer reference architecture (NL chatbot → rules engine → 7 agents) plus a delivery playbook for standing it up on a new commercial domain in < 12 weeks — with the audit trail, citation, and governance an enterprise CPG client expects.

Why this wins now

It is complementary to Liquid AI, not competitive. Liquid AI is the platform; we accelerate the persona-shaped surfaces (CPG Brand, Category, Retailer, Innovation, Customer Team) that a horizontal platform will always need on top.

02The Asset — A Three-Layer AI-for-BI Reference Tuned for CPG & GM

A working reference architecture across all three layers, configurable for any commercial domain that Circana tracks — food & beverage, beauty, healthcare, durables, tech, fashion, automotive aftermarket, foodservice.

Layer 1 · Conversational BI for Market Insights

Intent engine (LLM-powered) → query library against a CPG/GM semantic ontology (share, velocity, ACV, distribution, price-gap, promo lift, basket affinity) → auto-chosen visualization → AI-written narrative with the "so-what". Multi-turn memory; CPG-native synonym resolution ("velocity" / "rate of sale" / "ROS"); precise time-expression handling ("last 4 weeks", "L52W", "YTD vs YA").

Layer 2 · Policy / Rules-as-a-Service for Commercial Guardrails

A programmable rules engine that continuously evaluates live data against codified commercial policy — JBP commitments, retailer contract thresholds, distribution targets, MAP / MSRP guardrails, brand-health watchlists, promo-ROI floors, OSA service levels. Violations flow as ranked alerts with full evidence chain.

Layer 3 · Agentic Actions for Insights at Scale

Autonomous agents that monitor signals, draft artifacts, and propose actions with explicit human approval gates — a Share-Movement Watcher, an MBR / Category-Review Auto-Drafter, a Root-Cause Investigator, an Innovation / Pacesetter Watcher, a Retailer-Readiness Agent, an Insight-Distribution Agent, and a Pricing & Promo Investigator.

Tech stack (transferable)

FastAPI · LLM-agnostic (Groq, Azure OpenAI, Bedrock, or on-prem Llama) · DuckDB / Snowflake / BigQuery · Malloy semantic layer · Vega-Lite · immutable audit log.

What's already built

13 chart types, multi-domain query library, follow-up resolution, session memory, agent runtime with tool-calling, policy DSL, narrative generator — all domain-agnostic and reusable.

What we configure for Circana

The ontology (CPG/GM measures & dimensions), the query library, and the policy pack. Everything else is the same engine. That is why a 12-week vertical pilot is realistic, not aspirational.

05Positioning vs. Liquid AI — Complement, Don't Compete

Liquid AI is Circana's horizontal GenAI platform. The white space is the verticalized, persona-shaped analyst surfaces on top — a category that, by definition, a horizontal platform cannot fully ship itself.

Where Liquid AI sits

Data plane, retrieval, governance, LLM orchestration, the security boundary — the platform plumbing that every persona surface will build on. Strategic to Circana; not the place a partner should try to substitute.

Where we sit on top

Domain-specific intent engines, policy packs (JBP, MAP, distribution), ready-built agents per persona (Brand, Category, Retailer, Innovation, Customer Team), and the deliverable-quality narrative + audit layer that lets a Brand Director defend an insight to their CFO.

The one-sentence pitch to Circana

"You've built the platform — let us build the personas. We bring a proven AI-for-BI reference, port it onto Liquid Data and Liquid AI, and stand up two verticalized analyst experiences inside one quarter — defensible insights, auditable narratives, agent-driven artifacts, ready to monetize."
